Aqueous Extraction : Placer

Placer deposits are concentrations of heavy minerals, usually within loose alluvium that can easily be excavated and washed. Placer mining is used to exploit loosely consolidated deposits like common sand and gravel or gravels containing gold, tin, diamonds, platinum, titanium, or coal.
